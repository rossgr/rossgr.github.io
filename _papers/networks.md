## Abstract

This report presents the design, configuration, and evaluation of a networking solution for a small to medium-sized enterprise (SME). The proposed network includes fail over systems to minimize single points of failure, redundancy for reliability, and security features—such as Secure Shell (SSH) and guest wireless segmentation—to mitigate network attacks. Key components include routers, wireless segmentation, DHCP, HSRP, and SSH-based access control, to help the SME reach its business targets and operational requirements.
