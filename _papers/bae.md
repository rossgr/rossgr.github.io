## Abstract

This document details the necessary structure, organisational, technical, and industrial requirements of BAE Systems, in order to secure information assets from both digital and physical risk vectors. This is detailed by showing the different departments and individuals whose roles help define and enforce controls that help mitigate and protect assets from these known and unknown risks, and as a result prevent security incidents from occurring which could result in severe reputational, financial, and legal damages to the business. As the Chief Information Security Officer, (CISO), it is my responsibility to define the necessary policies and protocols to best manage BAE Systems extensive information assets and business operations from physical and cyber threats.
